Registration
Registration Guidelines
At least one author for every accepted paper is required to be registered for the conference for that paper to appear in the proceedings and be scheduled for presentation. Participating members may register as per the following charges:
Registration Fee
Authors 150 USD
Listener 100 USD
Online Registration
Delegates can register online for ICMSCI, using a secure credit/debit card payment site. Please note final documents without payment will not be accepted.
Registration fee includes the following,
Individual certificates for all the Authors, Bag, DVD Conference Proceedings, Conference Agenda, ID Card and Lanyard, Conference Pen and Scribbling Pad, Conference Presentation and Networking Opportunity, Refreshments Breaks (Morning/Evening), Lunch, Publication of 1 accepted paper in the proceedings.
Camera-Ready Submission Set
To confirm registration, the corresponding author needs to submit a Camera-ready set including:
  1. Final Updated Paper
  2. Payment Acknowledgement Screenshot
Registration Guidelines
  • Registered members should provide intimation about their payments immediately.
  • The corresponding authors of accepted papers are required to please fill, complete, sign and send the following Publishing Agreement along with your Camera Ready Submission to xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx@gmail.com. Failure to comply will result in the removal of the paper from the proceedings.
  • After completion of registration fee process, participant are required to send the scan copy of the registration fee receipt or transaction proof to us on or before the last date of registration.
  • Any modification in the paper will not be accepted after submitting the final documents.
  • 1 paper presentation and publication will be allowed per registration.
  • Authors of accepted papers can write / call coordinator as soon as possible, in case of registration beyond deadlines.
  • For further queries regarding the conference final documents, feel free to email us at xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx@gmail.com.
Cancellation & Refund Policy
After registration, no refunds or cancellations are permitted.
