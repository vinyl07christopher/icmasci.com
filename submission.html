Submission
ICMSCI 2024 encourages academicians, research scholars, and industrialists to submit their full papers, which would further undergo a double-blind peer review process. Kindly refer to the following guidelines before submitting your manuscript to the conference:
  • Each research paper must clearly address one or more of the topics included in ICMSCI 2024 Call for Papers.
  • Manuscripts contributed must not have previously been published, presented, or accepted for publication in any other conference or journal.
  • Multiple submissions from the same corresponding author will not be encouraged.
  • Maximum a manuscript can include 6 authors, wherein all author related information â€“ name, affiliation, and contact details should be mentioned only in the first page, and any information conveying identification of the author(s) must be removed from the manuscript.
  • The manuscript must be submitted in MS Word/LaTex compatible format text. The final manuscript length should be 12-15 pages.
  • The paper should not include any header or footer information in the top/bottom margins (e.g. do not include page numbers, copyright notices, etc.).
  • The authors should carefully proofread the manuscript before submission in order to eliminate the grammatical and typographical errors.
Papers should be submitted through email
  • [xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx@gmail.com] Authors are invited to submit original papers, which have not been published elsewhere and are not currently under consideration for another journal, conference or workshop.
  • Paper submissions should be limited to a maximum of 12-15 pages, including the bibliography and any possible appendices. Submissions longer than 15 pages and without specified template will be rejected without review. All submissions will be double-blind reviewed by the technical program committee on the basis of technical quality, relevance to scope of the conference, originality, significance, and clarity.
  • All the papers will be double blind reviewed by an expert committee with respect to their quality, originality, and relevance. Articles will be subject to plagiarism check before being sent for review process.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings provided at least one of the authors will register and present the paper. All rights of publication of papers presented in the conference shall rest with the Conference Organizers and Springer, the publication partner of the conference.
